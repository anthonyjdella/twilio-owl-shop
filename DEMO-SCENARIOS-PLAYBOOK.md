## 🛍️ E-commerce Customer Demo

```typescript

brandName: "Customer Store"
brandColors: { primary: "#E11D48" } // Customer's brand color

// Enable these features:
- ✅ Order confirmations
- ✅ Shipping updates  
- ✅ Cart abandonment recovery
- ✅ SMS media messages (product images)
- ✅ WhatsApp product catalogs
```

### **Demo Script (10 minutes)**

**Opening (1 min):**
*"Let me show you exactly how Customer Store would use Twilio to boost sales and customer satisfaction. I've customized this demo with your brand colors and messaging..."*

**Step 1: Complete Customer Journey (3 min)**
1. Click "🎭 Complete Customer Journey"
2. **Point to virtual phone**: *"And here's what your customers see on their actual phones..."*
3. Watch messages appear: Marketing → Cart Recovery → Order → Shipping → Feedback

**Step 2: Rich Product Showcase (3 min)**
1. Switch to **WhatsApp channel**
2. Click "WhatsApp List Messages" feature
3. **Show virtual phone**: *"Customers can browse your product categories directly in WhatsApp..."*
4. Click "WhatsApp Media Sharing" 
5. **Point out**: *"Rich product images with buy buttons right in the chat"*

**Step 3: Live Customization (2 min)**
1. Click **edit button (✏️)** on any message template
2. Change message content live: *"Want to customize for seasonal promotions? Watch this..."*
3. Update message: "🎄 Holiday Sale! Get 30% off Customer Store favorites"
4. **Send demo**: Shows updated message instantly

**Step 4: Multi-Channel Power (1 min)**
1. Switch channels: SMS → RCS → WhatsApp
2. **Emphasize**: *"Same message, different experience levels. Meet customers where they are."*

**Closing Questions:**

## 🎯 Demo Success Metrics

### **Engagement Indicators**
- Customer asks specific implementation questions
- Requests pricing or timeline information  
- Asks about integration with their existing systems
- Wants to schedule technical deep-dive

### **Common Follow-up Questions**
- *"How quickly can we get this set up?"*
- *"What's the pricing structure?"*
- *"Can this integrate with our CRM?"*
- *"What about international customers?"*
- *"How do we handle compliance requirements?"*

### **Demo Personalization Tips**
- Use customer's actual company name throughout
- Reference their specific industry challenges
- Show relevant competitor examples
- Mention their current communication tools
- Address their compliance requirements
